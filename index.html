True End-to-End Encryption

Under the hood
How a message travels
1
Register
Your nickname + X25519 pubkey stored on Qubic smart contract
2
Discover
Look up recipient's public key from the contract by nickname
3
Encrypt
Derive shared secret via X25519. Encrypt with XSalsa20-Poly1305
4
Deliver
Encrypted blob sent P2P via libp2p. Never touches a server
5
Prove
Content hash posted on-chain as tamper-proof delivery receipt
